What is a day like in receiving 311 calls?

We all have complains. Might be an auto-renewed membership, or neighbors party music. While 'Contact Us' isnt always an option, 311 seems to be there 24/7.

The following visualization is an abstraction of our complaints to 311, by breaking down numbers of service requests filterd by complaint types and responsible agencies, using data pulled from NYC Open Data.

Analyzing complaints is a traverse. In August, we see the city-proof life routine - Noise complaints filed to NYPD has always came out on top during midnight, especially the weekend's; And reports of noncompliance with phased opening to the Mayor's remained constant during dining hours. Also, we see the aftermath of tropical storm Isaias - Complaints of damaged tree spiked on August 4, after the storm whipping through NYC.
